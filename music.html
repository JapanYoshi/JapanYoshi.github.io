About

I was born in Hokkaidō, Japan in 1999 to achieve greatness. Either that or make some bangin' music. Either works.

I speak fluent English and Japanese, as well as conversational Spanish, and some Esperanto.

1999年、歴史に名を残すために北海道で生まれました。それかノリノリの音楽を作るためか。どっちでもいいや。

日本語と英語を流暢に話せます。スペイン語では会話をそこそこ続けられるレベルで、エスペラントも少し出来ます。

License

Noncommercial Use

Akira Sora & Szistirra’s music is released with the Creative Commons Attribution license. Akira Sora & Szistirra’s music can be used noncommercially with credit, with permission that will very likely be granted. Please contact me via my social links to let me know.

Commercial Use

Commercial use of Akira Sora & Szistirra’s music must be negotiated. Please contact me via my social links with a summary of your project and how you will use it. (If it’s for a rhythm game, I’ll probably say yes!)

Exceptions

Tracks which contain copyrighted material should not be used in purposes that involve distribution to the masses.
