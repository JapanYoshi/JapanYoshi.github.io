concept

STARLiGHT is a family of noteskins that is compatible with the Dance mode of Stepmania, including the Solo style.

Its modern design, with colors contrasting and readable against light or dark backgrounds, brings the beloved DDR arrow to the 2020s.

It boasts 7 different colorations for maximum compatibility. More on this below.

color coding

STARLIGHT-NOTE

Follows official DDR NOTE standard.

4th: red
8th: blue
16th: yellow
other: green

STARLIGHT-SMNOTE

Extends official DDR NOTE standard. Used by yuisin.

4th: red
8th: blue
12th: purple
16th: yellow
24th: pink
32nd: orange
48th: cyan
64th: green
other: gray

STARLIGHT-CEL

Follows In The Groove standard (albeit slightly modified).

4th: red
8th: blue
12th: purple
16th: green
24th: purple
32nd: yellow
48th: purple
other: gray

STARLIGHT-RAINBOW

Follows DDR RAINBOW standard.

on exact beat: red
before ¼ beat: pink
before ½ beat: blue
before ¾ beat: purple
other: red

STARLIGHT-CYCLE

Extends DDR RAINBOW standard to 12 timings.

on exact beat: red
1/6 to 1/4 beat: yellow
5/12 to 1/2 beat: aqua
2/3 to 3/4 beat: purple
other: in between

STARLIGHT-VIVID

Follows DDR VIVID standard. Notes cycle through rainbow color pattern, but starting at different times depending on its timing.

on exact beat: starting at yellow
before ¼ beat: starting at pink
before ½ beat: starting at purple
before ¾ beat: starting at cyan
other: starting at yellow

STARLIGHT-FLAT

Follows DDR FLAT standard. Notes cycle through rainbow color pattern regardless of its timing.

how to use

The STARLiGHT Noteskin’s 7 variations use common assets. STARLIGHT-NOTE is required to use any others (besides STARLIGHT-CYCLE).

To install the noteskin, follow these steps:

  1. Extract all folders to /Noteskins/dance. (If you are on Stepmania 5.3, extract to /Appearance/Noteskins/dance.)
  2. If Stepmania is running, reload metrics and textures using F2. If not, simply start Stepmania.

That is to say, the Noteskins folder should look like this:

  • Noteskins/
    • dance/
      • starlight-note/ (required)
        • metrics.ini
        • Noteskin.lua
        • image assets
      • starlight-vivid/
      • starlight-cel/
      • ...
